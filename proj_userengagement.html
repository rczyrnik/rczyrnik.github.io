What is the goal of this project?

I struggled with the nebulous goal of this project, which was ultimately "find something useful in this data". To focus my attention, I created two goals of my own.

  1. Predict who will respond to an email sent from another user.
  2. Predict which users are likely to engage with the app.

Context

This was the capstone project from my time at Galvanize. I worked on this project on my own, with some support from the company I partnered with.

What tools did I use?

I analyzed the data with python using pandas, numpy, and sklearn.

What was the big takeaway?

In the words of the SAT, "Work quickly but do not rush". I wasted a lot of time at the start because of disordered EDA, when really a straightforward, methodical workflow provided the most long-term benefits.
