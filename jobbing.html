Introduction

Qutting my job last summer was the scarriest thing I've done, but now that I've finished my program and looking for work, I'm strangly calm about the whole process.

Instead of making my goal "get hired", something which I have very little control over, my goal is "work full time applying to jobs", which is something I know I can make happen.

I'm actually really excited to have all this free time to work on the projects I've had to put off while in school. I want this blog to be a record of all the accomplishments I achieve during this unique opportunity.

February 12, Day 1

Even though graduation was two fridays ago, I wanted to give myself some time to mentally prepare for the job hunt. So I decided that, come hell or high water, I would start applying on Feb 12.

Well, it's good to start off with a success. I applied to my first few jobs this morning. I think it's probably better if I don't keep a public running tally, so from now on you can assume I'm sending in applications on the regular.
