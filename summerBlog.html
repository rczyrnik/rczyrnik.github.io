22 June 2017

I'm getting better at starting my day on time. This morning I was out of the house by 9, although that was to take Gandalf for a walk. I didn't reach the coffee shop until noon. Before you judge me too harshly, do remember that it is just my third day of vacation. Heaven forbid I should sleep in a little.

I got a time tracking program to help keep track of how I spend my day. You can see that most of my time is unaccounted for. This is because I only downloaded the app in the afternoon and I'm just figuring out how it works. But don't worry! I look forward to obsessively tracking my days to the minute.

My largest category today was "Stuff" which is short for "Stuff that needs doing". Today that was mostly health stuff like making appointments (haven't had a primary care physician since my pediatrician kicked me out at 18) and panicking that my healthcare spending account expired on Tuesday and I just lost $300 in unspent money (I actually have until September 18, but my employer is changing providers). General adult stuff.

Being able to log the time I spent on "stuff" was very validating because it feels like such a waste of time, which it is, but now I can say that I got 2 hrs 23 min of it done with today.

On the productive side of things, I'm starting to feel like SQL kind of makes sense, which is good. I finished another Codecademy SQL course today. Two down, one more to go!
