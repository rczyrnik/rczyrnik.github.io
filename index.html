Hello! I am a Data Science Resident at Galvanize in San Francisco. My primary role is to help students succeed at the 12 week data science intensive program, although I also do work supporting the instructors, as well as teaching some evening python and stats programs.

A bit about me! I graduated from Yale with a degree in physics and then moved to Korea to teach English as a Second Language. After three years away from home, I returned to Connecticut and earned a Masters in Teaching. I taught physics at a public high school in Fairfield County, but before long I started looking for a greater challenge.

Last fall I moved to San Francisco to persue a career in tech. I emrolled in Galvanize's 12 week intensitve data science program (the same one I'm working for now). It's been an amazing journey, but it's not over yet.

I put together this webpage to practice HTML and CSS. I know it may be simple compared to profesionally-designed sites, but it was made from scratch with love. It is both a project and a repository for my projects, which is pretty meta! I hope you like it.
